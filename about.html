OVER FIVE DECADES OF FIRE FIGHTING EQUIPMENT & SYSTEMS

Muthu Fire has been a pioneer in the fire protection industry for the past 50 years. Established in 1969, our company has been dedicated to providing top-notch fire safety equipment and systems to our valued customers.

We specialize in manufacturing and supplying a diverse range of fire protection products, including Fire Extinguishers, Fire Detection and Alarm Systems, Fire Hydrant Systems, Fire Suppression Systems, and more.

At Muthu Fire, we understand the critical importance of fire safety, and our products are designed to meet and exceed global industry standards. Our team of experts ensures that our products are thoroughly tested and evaluated before they are released into the market.

Our commitment to quality, reliability, and affordability has helped us earn the trust and loyalty of our customers. We take pride in delivering world-class products that meet our customer's expectations and requirements.

Our core values include integrity, professionalism, and customer satisfaction. We believe in building long-term relationships with our customers and strive to provide exceptional service and support.

Over the years, Muthu Fire has grown from strength to strength, and our products are now available across India. We continue to invest in research and development to enhance our product range and provide innovative fire safety solutions to our customers.

We cater to all major Government & Department, Public & Private sector, Educational Institutions, Electrical & Electronics Industries, Petroleum Industries, Farms, Food, Rubber, Paper, Textiles Mills, Power stations, Hotels, Malls, Jewellery's, Clubs, Shopping Complexes & so on.
